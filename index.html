Royal Orchard Middle School

'“Collaborate, Believe, Succeed” Royal Orchard Middle School is located in northwest Brampton. We are committed to providing a healthy and positive learning environment to support student success. Teachers at Royal Orchard are dedicated to engaging our students in the learning process by providing them with rich, personally relevant learning experiences with a strong focus on our 4 A’s: Academics, The Arts, Athletics and Aspiring Attitude. Royal Orchard Middle School is a place where students are always encouraged to develop leadership skills as well as a positive self image that will maximize their potential as critical thinkers and learners while participating as active and positive citizens in the community.'

School News

  • Provincial government extends remote learning period in Peel until Feb. 10, 2021

    The Government of Ontario announced today, Jan. 12, 2021, that elementary and secondary (K-12) students in Peel will continue to learn remotely until Feb. 10, 2021. School routines for students in the Online Learning Model will continue as usual.

  • Peel Public Health letter re: changes to COVID-19 screening

    To help further reduce the spread of COVID-19 in our school communities, Peel Public Health has issued a letter with important changes to COVID-19 screening. Starting Dec. 7, 2020, all children with at least one symptom of COVID-19, even mild symptoms, must stay home, self-isolate and are recommended to get tested.

  • Apply for the Ministry of Education’s Support for Learners

    The Ministry of Education is providing financial support for families during this temporary remote learning period through the Support for Learners program. Click Full Story to learn more and apply.
